Research

My research interests are Artificial Intelligence, Instructable Agents, Cybersecurity, Adversary Persistent Threats, Knowledge Engineering, Evidence-Based Reasoning, Machine Learning, Intelligence Analysis, Cognitive Assistants, Expert Systems.
My current research mainly focuses on building Instructable Agents for Advanced Persistent Threats (APT) Detection.
